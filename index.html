About VAT (Value Added Tax)

VAT is a tax applied to goods and services in Nepal. The standard VAT rate is 13%, which means for every product or service purchased, 13% is added to the price as tax. This is a consumption tax levied at each stage of the production and distribution chain.

In Nepal, businesses collect VAT on behalf of the government. They are required to remit the VAT collected to the government on a regular basis, typically monthly or quarterly, depending on their size and the amount of business they do.

VAT बारेमा (Value Added Tax)

नेपालमा मूल्य अभिवृद्धि कर (VAT) वस्तु र सेवामा लागु हुने कर हो। नेपालको मानक VAT दर १३% हो, जसको अर्थ हरेक वस्तु वा सेवाको मुल्यमा १३% थप गरिन्छ। यो एक उपभोक्ता कर हो, जुन उत्पादन र वितरण चक्रको प्रत्येक चरणमा लागु हुन्छ।

VAT Calculation Guide

To calculate VAT, follow these steps:

  • 1. Start with the base amount or price of the product or service.
  • 2. Multiply the base amount by the VAT rate (13% in Nepal).
  • 3. The result of this multiplication is the VAT amount.
  • 4. To find the total amount including VAT, add the VAT amount to the base amount.

For example: If you have an amount of NPR 100,000 and you need to add VAT (13%):

VAT = 100,000 × 0.13 = 13,000

Total Amount = 100,000 + 13,000 = 113,000
